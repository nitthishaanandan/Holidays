Types Of Holidays


Package Holidays


A holiday organized by a travel company for which you pay a fixed price that includes the cost of the hotel and travel, and sometimes food.

Independent Holidays


A holiday that you celebrate by yourself including making the arrangements and other things.

Domestic Holidays


Domestic holiday is a holiday or a vacation spent in the same country or region; this class may overlap with staycation.

In-Bound and Out-Bound Holidays


Inbound tourism is when someone/non-resident visits a country. Outbound tourism is when someone goes out of their own country, it can be for holidays, business, trip etc. It is like immigration and emigration.

Religious Holidays


Religious holiday is a day specified for religious observances. These are holidays that are linked to faiths and religions. This holds especially true for those holidays that have combined and absorbed rituals.

All Inclusive Holidays


All-inclusive is used to indicate that a price, especially the price of a holiday, includes all the charges and all the services offered.
